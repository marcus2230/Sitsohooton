Sitshoothon, “The Student from Shoothon” – named after the Shoothon Muay Thai gym owned by the great Rodriguez brothers, is a gym based in Banksia, Sydney that specializes in the art of the 8 limbs, being Muay Thai and the pugilistic form of Boxing.

Our trainers who are specialists in their disciplines teach innovative ways, without compromising honorable tradition that bring out the best in individuals – whether it be preparation for a fight, increasing fitness, weight loss or just accomplish personal goals, our trainers are there for you. Not only have they extensive experience as trainers but also they have real fight experience so therefore they know what they are teaching.

We encourage a ‘no ego’ environment and instill a community of members who always look out for each other.

Our doors are always open to those eager to learn the art of Muay Thai and Boxing who seek the above values.
